A flat-screen TV with satellite channels and DVD player, as well as an iPad are featured. Some units have a seating area. Some rooms feature views of the sea or pool. Slippers and a hairdryer can be found in the private bathroom. Some rooms include a spa bath or a hot tub.
Guests may relax in the luxurious 2000-square-metre lobby, take a dip in the swimming pool or play a game of tennis with travel mates. Meeting and banquet facilities are provided upon request.
Authentic local delicacies can be enjoyed at the on-site Chinese restaurant, while Western gourmet is served at the Western restaurant.
